	- Configurar la conexion a internet: sudo nano /etc/netplan/00-installer-config.yaml

            enp0s3: 
	      addresses: [192.168.210.239/24] 
	      gateway4: 192.168.210.1 
	      nameservers: 
		addresses: [192.168.210.1, 8.8.8.8]
